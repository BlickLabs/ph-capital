Our Philosophy

PH Capital participates in real estate deals mainly through equity stakes. The Firm seeks to participate at early stage of a development: land acquisition. This approach allows PH Capital to participate in the definition of the business plans and predevelopment activities seeking to create superior value through strategy and planning.
