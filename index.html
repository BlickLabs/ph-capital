Investment Strategy - THESIS

Cities, not countries, are the constituent elements of a developing economy and have been so from the dawn of civilization. PH Capital believes urbanization and high dense cities create an enormous variety of opportunities to develop and finance real estate products.

/02

Investment
Strategy -
Our Philosophy

PH Capital participates in real estate deals mainly through equity stakes. The Firm seeks to participate at early stage of a development: land acquisition. This approach allows PH Capital to participate in the definition of the business plans and predevelopment activities seeking to create superior value through strategy and planning.
/03

SMART
INVESTMENTS

We strongly believe that the best way to achieve superior value to our stake holders is through sustained and long-term returns avoiding unnecessary risks. Through our risk adjusted return perspective we preserve the capital of our sponsors and maximize the returns of our investments.
